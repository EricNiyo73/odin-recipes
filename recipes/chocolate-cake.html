Chocolate Cake

Description

This moist and decadent chocolate cake is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Baking powder
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vegetable oil
  • Vanilla extract
  • Boiling water

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease the cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the dry ingredients: flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract to the bowl and mix until well combined.
  4. Slowly pour in the boiling water while stirring the batter.
  5. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared cake pans.
  6. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Let the cakes cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Frost the cakes with your favorite frosting and enjoy!
