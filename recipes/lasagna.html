Lasagna

Description

This is a classic Italian lasagna recipe made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles until al dente.
  2. In a skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic until the meat is browned.
  3. Add tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper to the skillet and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese, shredded mozzarella, and grated Parmesan.
  5. Layer the ingredients in a baking dish: meat sauce, noodles, cheese mixture, and repeat.
  6. Bake in the oven at 350°F (175°C) for 30 minutes.
  7. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
