Pizza

Description

This homemade pizza recipe will satisfy your cravings for a cheesy and flavorful pizza.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushrooms
  • Green bell pepper
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to the highest temperature.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a greased baking sheet or pizza stone.
